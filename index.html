ABOUT US

If we give a little love, may be we can change the world

- Ariana Grande

Born on 26th June 1993 in Baco Raton, Florida,United states, Ariana Grande-Butera had grew up to be a very extremely talented singer, dancer and as well as a beautiful actress. Ariana's parents had moved away from New York to florida just before she was born where her mum Joan Grande who owns a company that manufactures communications equipment for the Navy & Marine and has always been a massive influencer in ArianaGrande's career and dad Edward Butera who was a Graphic Designer and the founder of IBI Designs Inc was said to be split when little ariana was just 8 years old. She often refers her self as an Italian American of "Half Scilian & Half Abruzzese" descent. Her older half brother's name is Frankie Grande who is an entertainer and producer and is ten years older than her. She attended the Pine Crest School and North Broward Prepatory School, how ever she left school to persue her music career but it was said that she continued to stay enrolled where her tutors would send her the materials to help her study. Her love for music started from day one and has never chaged over the years. At age 8 she performed at a Karoke lounge on a cruise ship where she made her first national television appearance singing "The Star-Spangled Banner" for the Florida Panthers. She then started her career when she was only 15 years old where Millions of fans fell in love with her as a singer and as well as an actress with her role as Cat Valentine in the Nickelodeon television series Victorious and Sam & Cat. She even took part in the musical cuba libre which was produced by the American songwritter Desmond Child where she played the role of Miriam. During the filming of Victirious ,Ariana made several songs covers of other artists namingly Adele, WhitneyHouston and Mariah Carey and uploaded them to YouTube where a friend of the CEO of Republic Records Monte Lipman was impressed by Ariana's vocals and sent the links to lipman who then signed her to a recording contract. Ariana has released altogether 6 studio albums, 1 compilation album, 2 extended plays (EP's), 50 singles which includes 14 as a featured artist, 1 live album, 1 remix album, 11 promotional singles and 40 music videos. Where she won 130 awards with 413 nominations. To date, she has amassed over 5 million Twitter followers, 3 million Facebook likes, 3 million Instagram followers, and 150 million YouTube/VEVO views. She has appeared in commercials for Macy's, T-Mobile, and Apple, as well as for her own fragrances. Where as Ariana has also released 7 fragrances with Luxe Brands. She Launched her debut fragrance, Ari by Ariana Grande in 2015 , when this got success, she launched the rest of the collection namingly Sweet Like Candy, Moonlight, Cloud, Thank U next ,limited edition fragrances Frankie and Sweet Like Candy Limited Edition. Her fragrance has also won the Frangrance Foundation Award multiple times. Ultimately Ariana has won the hearts of many people, where her albums will bring everyone to Ariana's world and it's wonderful place.
